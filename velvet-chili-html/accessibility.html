Accessibility Statement

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Our Commitment

Velvet Chili is dedicated to providing an inclusive online experience for all visitors, regardless of ability. We are continually working to improve the accessibility of our website to ensure equal access to our menu, reservations, and restaurant information.

Conformance Status

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities, and we regularly review our site against these standards.

Measures We Take

We implement the following practices to maintain accessibility:

  • Semantic HTML structure for assistive technologies
  • Proper heading hierarchy and descriptive link text
  • Keyboard-navigable menus and forms
  • Alt text for all meaningful images
  • Sufficient colour contrast across all text
  • ARIA labels where necessary

Limitations & Feedback

We recognise that some areas may not yet be fully accessible. We are actively working to address these. If you encounter any barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please let us know. We value your feedback.

Contact Us

For accessibility inquiries, please contact our team at accessibility@velvetchili.com or call (555) 123-4567. We will respond as quickly as possible.
